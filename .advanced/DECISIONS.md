# DECISIONS.md

## Purpose
Track important decisions, alternatives considered, and the reasoning behind them, in a lightweight ADR-style format.

## Decision Log

### [YYYY-MM-DD] Decision Title
- **Context**: Brief description of the situation or problem.
- **Decision**: What you chose.
- **Alternatives Considered**: Short list of other options and why they were not selected.
- **Rationale**: Key reasons for the decision.
- **Status**: Proposed / Accepted / Superseded.

Repeat this section for each significant decision.
