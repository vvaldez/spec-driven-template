# VERBOSITY.md

## Purpose
Define how long and detailed you generally want AI responses to be, and when to deviate from that default.

## Default Verbosity
Specify your usual preference, such as:
- Very concise (just the essentials).
- Moderate detail (short explanations with examples).
- High detail (step-by-step reasoning and thorough context).

## When to Be More Detailed
Describe situations where you want richer explanations, for example:
- When discussing architecture or trade-offs.
- When learning a new concept for the first time.

## When to Be Extra Terse
Describe situations where you want extremely short answers, for example:
- When you are on mobile with limited time.
- When you are executing a plan and just need quick confirmations.

You can also note that mobile or voice-first use (for example, when you are walking or in line) should default to terse responses unless you explicitly ask for more detail.
